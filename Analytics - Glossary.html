Glossary

Showing for:

All Modules

Absenteeism

Unauthorized or excessive employee absence impacting productivity and workflow.

Availability

Employee presence, expressed as hours, shifts, or specific timeframes, when they are ready and able to work.

Exits

Employees leaving the organization permanently, impacting workforce composition and talent retention.

Headcount

The number of active employees.

Hires

New employees who join the organization permanently within a specified timeframe, impacting workforce growth and talent acquisition.

Holiday

The number of holidays in the period under review.

Leave

Refers to authorized employee absences from work, encompassing various types.

Organizational Level

Hierarchical method of classifying employee's position within the company's structure.

Performance Rate

Refers to the formal or informal assessment of an employee's job performance against pre-defined criteria or expectations.

Promotion

Refers to the movement of an employee to a higher position or rank within the organization's hierarchy.

Salary

Refers to the fixed amount of money an employee receives regularly, typically monthly or annually, for their work.
